\section{Related work}
\label{sec:related}

Adaptive resampling machine-learned
models~\cite{ml_resampling_2025,ml_fullcore_2026} retain
$10$--$50\%$ of the pointwise data with $\leq 97$~pcm
criticality error. Our approach is orthogonal (replace the
representation, not subsample the array). Windowed Multipole
for Doppler broadening is due to
\cite{josey2015wmp,forget2014wmp}; hybrid multipole + windowed
networks in RMC code~\cite{hybrid_wn_2021} pair multipole with
a second analytical method; our hybrid pairs multipole with
SVD. Event-based GPU transport follows the Tramm
architecture~\cite{tramm2024} and shares the memory-bottleneck
diagnosis of earlier XSBench studies~\cite{tramm2014xsbench}.
Cross-isotope basis sharing was investigated and found not
viable beyond the first singular vector (subspace angle
$>\!85^\circ$ at $k=2$ between $^{235}$U, $^{238}$U,
$^{239}$Pu), a finding that echoes KV-cache compression
experience in a different domain~\cite{minicache2024}.
